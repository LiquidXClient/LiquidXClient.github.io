How to install LiquidX

  • Step 1: Download and install Minecraft Forge 1.8.9 : click here
  • Step 2: Download LiquidX : click here
  • Step 3: Press the Windows + R key
  • Step 4: Enter this path %appdata%/.minecraft/mods in this folder you will place LiquidX 3.1
  • Step 5: Restart Minecraft and the mod should be installed and ready to use!


How to install ViaVersion for LiquidX

  • Step 1: You must already have LiquidX installed by following the steps above.
  • Step 2: Download ViaVersion : click here
  • Step 3: Press the Windows + R key
  • Step 4: Enter this path %appdata%/.minecraft/mods in this folder you will place ViaVersion
  • Step 5: Start your Minecraft as you normally do when running LiquidX
